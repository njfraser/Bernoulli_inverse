This folder contains data presented in Fraser and Cunningham (2021) submitted to GRL.

Contents:

AMOC_timeseries.mat	-Monthy mean AMOC from the BI applied to EN4 at 50N
fluxes.mat		-Monthly mean heat, freshwater and mass flux from the BI applied to EN4 at 50N
deta.mat		-Sea surface slope as function of time and longitude at 50N from BI applied to EN4
OTSF.mat		-Overturning streamfunction in neutral density space, as a function of time from the BI applied to EN4 at 50N
